I generally share source code, our group often has vacancies for graduate students, we encourage visitors, and I am happy to answer research-related questions. If I am not travelling, then I am available on short notice, but by appointment only (via email).


Map by MazeMap

You can find our research group in IT-bygget on the Gløshaugen campus (main campus) of NTNU in Trondheim, Norway. I am in room 207.

From airport Værnes: Immediately outside arrivals, catch the blue “Flybussen” express coach (with views of the fjord) to the “Studentersamfunnet” stop. The coach will depart every fifteen minutes and arrive after about forty minutes. “Studentersamfunnet” is a large, red, round building at the bottom of the main hill to campus. Walk up “Høgskoleveien” alongside the south of “Studentersamfunnet” to reach the main library at the primary entrance to campus. Continue straight on to reach IT-Bygget in the back of the campus (about five minutes farther).
